Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for Saudi Pro League club Al-Nassr and the Portugal national team. Often considered the best player in the world and widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time, Ronaldo has won five Ballon d'Or awards and four European Golden Shoes, the most by a European player.

Who is CR7?

Cristiano Ronaldo is known as CR7 because this combines his initials and his iconic jersey number, 7. Throughout his career at Manchester United, Real Madrid, Juventus, and with the Portugal national team, Ronaldo has frequently worn the number 7. This number is historically significant, having been worn by many legendary players before him, making it even more meaningful and synonymous with his image and personal brand. One of Ronaldo's most iconic celebrations is the "Siu" (pronounced "See-ew") celebration, where he jumps high into the air, spins around, and spreads his arms wide while shouting "Siu!" This celebration is adored by fans worldwide.

Cristiano Ronaldo, born on February 5, 1985, in Madeira, Portugal, is a legendary footballer known for his incredible skill, speed, and goal-scoring ability. He has played for top clubs and has won numerous titles, including multiple Ballon d'Or awards. Often considered one of the GOATs (Greatest of All Time) in football , Ronaldo's impact on the sport is immense, competing with contemporaries like Lionel Messi for the title of the best footballer of their era.

Clubs

Sporting CP

2002-2003

Ronaldo began his professional career at Sporting CP in Portugal, where he quickly caught the attention of top clubs with his exceptional talent. He played for Sporting CP from 2002 to 2003 before moving to Manchester United.

Mancaster United

2003-2009

At Manchester United, Ronaldo became a global superstar, winning multiple Premier League titles, the UEFA Champions League, and his first Ballon d'Or award. He played for Manchester United from 2003 to 2009, This was the beginning of his era.

Real Madrid

2009-2018

Ronaldo joined Real Madrid in 2009, where he further solidified his status as one of the greatest footballers of all time. He won multiple La Liga titles, Champions League titles, and Ballon d'Or awards during his time at Real Madrid.

Juventus

2018-2021

Ronaldo joined Juventus in 2018, bringing his goal-scoring prowess to the Serie A. He has continued to excel at Juventus, which moves to serie a and got winning Serie A titles and further solidifying his status as one of the greatest footballers of all time.

Mancaster United

2021-2022

Ronaldo returned to Manchester United in 2021, reuniting with the club where he first made his mark on the football world. His return to Manchester United has been highly anticipated by fans and has reignited his career in the Premier League.

Al-Nassr

2023-now

Ronaldo joined Al-Nassr in 2022, bringing his talents to the Saudi Pro League. His move to Al-Nassr has been a new chapter in his illustrious career, showcasing his ability to compete at the highest level in different league and for a very fantastic fee.

Awards

Ballon d'Or

5 Times

Ronaldo has won the prestigious Ballon d'Or award five times, recognizing him as the best player in the world. His Ballon d'Or wins have solidified his status as one of the greatest footballers of all time, being hold the number 2 position for the most trophies.

European Golden Shoe

4 Times

Ronaldo has won the European Golden Shoe award four times, recognizing him as the top goal-scorer in European leagues. His Golden Shoe wins highlight his incredible goal-scoring ability and consistency throughout his career, being hold the number 2 position for the most.

UEFA Champions League

5 Times

Ronaldo has won the UEFA Champions League five times, showcasing his dominance in European club football. His Champions League wins include titles with both Real Madrid and Manchester United, further solidifying his legacy in the sport.

UEFA Euro Championship

1 Once

Ronaldo has won the UEFA European Championship once with the Portugal national team. His captain and goal-scoring prowess were instrumental in Portugal's victory in the 2016 UEFA European Championship, marking a historic moment in his career.
